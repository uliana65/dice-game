DICE

RULES

Take turns with Computer to roll 6 dice. After a roll, the
scoring dice are tallied and set aside. You can then either:

a) finish your turn and add your points to your game score, or
b) roll the remaining dice to try and score more points.

If all 6 dice score points, you can still try and score more
by rolling all the six dice again. If none of your dice score
points, you lose ALL your points from THAT turn and your
turn is over.

NOTE! The scoring combinations must be rolled in one throw,
meaning you cannot re-use results from earlier throws. You
win the game if you score more points than Computer.
The game is over when a player gains 500 points.
